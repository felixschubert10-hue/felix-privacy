Privacy Policy – EngagementPump

Last updated:

EngagementPump (“the App”) is a tool that helps creators manage Instagram interactions (e.g., auto-replies to comments/DMs, moderation, and scheduled publishing). This policy explains what data we process, why, and how you can exercise your rights.

Who we are

Controller: Felix Schubert
Email: felixschubert10@gmail.com

Data we access/process

  • Instagram account identifiers that you connect to the App (Page ID, Instagram User ID).
  • Public interactions the App receives via Meta Webhooks and APIs: comments, comment metadata, basic profile username of the commenter, direct messages you receive, and message metadata.
  • Content you choose to schedule/publish through the App (media URL, caption, first comment, publish time).
  • Technical logs needed to operate and secure the service (timestamps, event types, delivery status, errors).

How we use the data

  • To detect intent (e.g., question/praise/toxicity) and send your configured auto-replies.
  • To moderate interactions you enable (e.g., hide toxic comments).
  • To schedule and publish content on your connected Instagram account(s).
  • To provide basic analytics and reliability (event logs, success/failure).

Legal basis

We process data you connect to the App based on your consent and our legitimate interest in providing the service you request.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers strictly necessary to run the App (e.g., Meta Platforms for Instagram Graph API, hosting providers). These providers process data under contractual safeguards.

Retention

Operational logs and interaction records are kept for up to 180 days by default, or less if you delete them sooner. Scheduled posts are kept until published and then archived for your records unless you delete them.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data at any time by emailing felixschubert10@gmail.com. You can also disconnect the App’s access to your accounts in Facebook/Instagram settings.

Security

We use HTTPS, access tokens, and least-privilege access. Secrets are stored as environment variables. No payment data is processed by the App.

Data deletion

To delete data processed by EngagementPump, email felixschubert10@gmail.com with the subject “Data Deletion – EngagementPump”. We will delete App-stored data associated with your accounts and confirm the deletion. You can also revoke the App’s permissions in Facebook → Settings → Business Integrations, and Instagram → Settings → Apps and Websites.

Children

The App is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as our service evolves. We will post changes here and update the “Last updated” date.
